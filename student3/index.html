The Embrabelle Café provides a calm and safe environment for your enjoyment. Located a short 5 minute walk from the Halifax Harbor, we offer a unique experience tailored to those seeking an escape from day-to-day stresses. Founded in 2012, we will continue to do our best in helping you do your best!

Why the library? Our goal is to lend a comfort to anyone, whether you are in person with us, or at home in your own space. The library system allows us to be more than just a stand-alone café - we try to make a lasting impact on our customers.

Our highly qualified staff are committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming environment for anyone to enjoy. Our company's mission is to provide quality services such as custom coffee, handmade pastries or book related needs. We try our best to offer extended hours to accommodate all schedules, along with fair rates for coffee and snacks, which you can browse on our menu. If you're still skeptical, you can read about what some of our satisfied customers have to say. Please don't hesitate to fill out our inquiries form if you have any further questions. If you're interested in similar shops like ours, you can check them out on our links page.
